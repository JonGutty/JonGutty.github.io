About

Greetings from Jon Gutierrez!
Here is a little background info, being born and raised in Fresno, Ca. I grew up with many challenges and hardships that gave me a unique outlook on life. 2011 marked the year in which I left Fresno to persue my dream of living in San Francisco, during these last 9 years I have had a wide range of jobs and lived in various neighborhoods here in the city. My favorite job to date was working as a bike courier for local company called "TCB Courier", sadly that company is no longer operating but I was able to meet and befriend people I wouldn't have met otherwise. Now after living in almost every neighborhood I've found myself always returning to the exact same area I first arrived when relocating to the city, which is the Inner Sunset neighborhood. With tons of delicious eateries and quaint cafes on various corners in the Inner Sunset this area will always be a special place to me.
